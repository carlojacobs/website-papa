---
title: Four Chairs and a Window
created: 2025-12-11
summary: A note about old furniture, family habits, and why useful things earn the right to stay.
---
There are four dining chairs in this house that do not match.

One is too dark, one is slightly taller than the others, and one still creaks no matter how often it is tightened. Together, however, they are perfect. They have all survived meals, arguments, birthdays, and long evenings where nobody quite wanted to go home yet.

A matching set would be more impressive for visitors, but I do not think it would be better. These chairs tell the truth about a family: things arrive at different times, wear differently, and remain because they are still doing their job.

The same is true of people, now that I think of it.

I would rather live with sturdy, slightly imperfect things than replace them with polished objects that have never seen a proper winter.
